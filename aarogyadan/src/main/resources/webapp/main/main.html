  • WHAT WE ARE DOING?
    Contribute to remove all illness from society.

    Healthy mind resides in healthy body. Nothing is more important in life than health. If one is healthy/ fit, he or she can work hard for his or her family and can carry out their duties relentlessly. But if a person in a family gets seriously ill, then the whole family gets disturbed; its economic condition falls down. There are innumerable families in our country whose lives become wretched because of financial crisis caused by illnesses or diseases. Often, the illness is not serious initially and hence it is neglected but later it turns fatal. The family, then, cannot afford the expenses on the treatment; whatever savings it has is spent on it and the family goes bankrupt. The sickness destroys the family’s financial condition so badly that it never comes on track.
